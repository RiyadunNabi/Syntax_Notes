### AutoPause Firefox Extension: Complete Guide & Setup

### 💡 What is AutoPause?

**AutoPause** is a smart media management extension for Firefox. Its main job is to prevent audio chaos when you have multiple tabs open.

*   **The Problem it Solves:** If you are listening to music on Spotify or a video on YouTube, and you open a new tab to watch a Twitter/X video, both audios will play simultaneously, creating a noisy mess.
*   **What AutoPause Does:** The moment audio starts playing in your active tab, AutoPause **automatically pauses** the music or video playing in your background tabs. When you switch back to your original tab, it automatically resumes your music.

* * *

### 📥 Step 1: How to Download and Install

1.  Open your Firefox browser.
2.  Go directly to the official Firefox Add-ons Marketplace.
3.  In the search bar at the top, type **AutoPause** and press `Enter`.
4.  Click on the **AutoPause** extension from the search results (look for the infinity symbol `∞` logo).
5.  Click the blue **Add to Firefox** button.
6.  A popup will appear at the top right of your browser asking for permissions. Click **Add**.

* * *

### ⚙️ Step 2: How to Set Up Your Preferences

Once installed, you need to configure which sites it should control.

1.  Click the **Extensions button** (puzzle piece icon) in your top-right Firefox toolbar.
2.  Click the gear icon next to **AutoPause**, or click on it and select **Options/Preferences**.
3.  You will see the settings screen. Configure it using these preferred target settings:

### 1\. Places to use this extension

Type the websites where you want AutoPause to actively look for media. Separated by spaces.

*   *Example:* `https://www.youtube.com/* https://spotify.com*`
*   *(The `*` is a wildcard meaning it applies to any video or link on that specific website).*

### 2\. Exclude Matches

Type the websites where AutoPause should **never** interfere (like communication apps where you don't want your voice calls randomly paused). Separated by spaces.

*   *Example:* `https://discord.com/* https://meet.google.com/* https://teams.live.com/*`

### 3\. Feature Checkboxes

*   **Ignore media from other tabs:** Check this if you want background tabs to completely ignore your active tab's media.
*   **Ignore short media under 3 seconds:** Check this so short notification alerts (like a Facebook message ping) don't pause your 2-hour podcast.
*   **Disable automatic resume:** Check this if you want background videos to *stay paused* even when you click back onto their tab.

* * *

### ⚠️ Step 3: Saving Your Work (Crucial)

This extension does not have a traditional "Save" button.

*   **To Save:** After you type your website URLs or toggle any checkbox, click your mouse cursor back inside one of the text boxes and **press the `Enter` key** on your keyboard.
*   If you do not press `Enter`, Firefox will discard your changes the moment you close the tab.
